Welcome

Flexible HR Limited is a human resources consultancy providing professional support to small and medium-sized businesses as well as specific projects for both the public and private sector.

Our team of experienced experts provide a flexible service keeping you up-to-date with the constant changes in Employment Law such as Minimum Wage and Maternity Legislation.

We offer free consultations including an Employment Audit and recommendations of services Flexible HR can provide your business.

For a small monthly fee Flexible HR can also provide an ongoing professional service including relevant documentation to meet your business needs, a telephone helpline service and regular company visits.
