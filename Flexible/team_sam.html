Samantha Mott Chartered MCIPD, MSSP

Samantha Mott is a Co-founder and Director of Flexible HR Limited and has 20 years experience working in human resources within both private and public sector organisations. She is a Chartered Member of the Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (MCIPD) and a Member of the Specialist Society of Paralegals in employment law (MSSP).

Sam is qualified to Level 1 and 2 to deliver Saville and Holdsworth ability and psychometric tests as well as possessing three health and safety qualifications. Sam specialised in recruitment for three years for a large blue chip company and a further three years in an HR/Health and Safety Consultancy working for a variety of small/medium-sized companies. Her other experience includes employee relations, compensation and reward, recruitment and change management.
